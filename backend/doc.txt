Chiropractor Referral Network 

Functional Specification & Development Scope 

1. System Overview 

Purpose of the Platform 

The platform is a referral marketplace connecting patients seeking chiropractic care with chiropractors within the network. 

Patients submit referral requests through a public referral form. 

The platform evaluates all chiropractors based on predefined eligibility rules and determines which practitioners should be able to view the referral opportunity. 

Unlike a traditional referral assignment model, referrals are not automatically assigned to a single practitioner. 

Instead: 

Multiple eligible chiropractors may view the referral. 

Patient contact details remain hidden. 

Chiropractors can review referral details and decide whether they wish to claim the referral. 

The first eligible chiropractor to successfully claim the referral receives exclusive ownership. 

A lead token is deducted at the time of claim. 

Patient contact information is then unlocked for the claiming practitioner. 

All other practitioners immediately lose access to the referral. 

The platform generates revenue through subscription plans and lead-token purchases while also providing referral tracking, practitioner performance monitoring, patient feedback collection, compliance management, and business analytics.
